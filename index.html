Phase 1
Clocking & shift reminders
WhatsApp-based attendance — immediate impact
What we deliver
✅
Instant roll call at shift start
Automated WhatsApp check-in the moment a shift begins. No-shows flagged in seconds.
🔔
Pre-shift reminders
30–60 min before shift. Reduces no-shows before they happen.
📊
Live attendance dashboard
Real-time visibility across all 500 sites. Who's on, who's late, who's missing.
Problems eliminated
✕ 90-minute blind spot before anyone notices a no-show
✕ No visibility into who's actually on site
✕ Fraudulent time claims (5 min/guard/day = R7.5M/yr)

Phase 2
AI calls & auto-replacement
Eliminate manual scrambling entirely
What we deliver
🤖
AI calls to no-show guards
Automated voice calls to absent guards. Escalation if unanswered. Zero human effort.
⚡
Automated shift replacement
Offers shifts to nearest, appropriately skilled, available guards. Fair and instant.
📈
Escalation workflows
Area managers only involved when the system can't resolve it. Exception-based management.
Problems eliminated
✕ 400–600 manual calls per day (13–20 hrs of staff time)
✕ Control room & area manager scrambling
✕ Sites left uncovered during replacement search
R2M–R3.5M
Annual savings from
replacement reduction & admin time reclaimed
